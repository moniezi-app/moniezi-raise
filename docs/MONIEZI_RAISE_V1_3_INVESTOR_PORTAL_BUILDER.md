# MONIEZI Raise V1.3 — Investor Portal Builder

## Purpose

V1.3 changes MONIEZI Raise from a generic raise/signup app into a practical investor/supporter portal builder.

The buyer can now use the app to create a polished private funding page similar in purpose to a small investor portal:

- business story
- funding goal
- use of funds
- proof/readiness points
- terms or support structure summary
- private contact audience
- next-step process
- non-binding indication-of-interest form
- downloadable supporter/investor package
- static portal HTML export

## Two templates

V1.3 supports two buyer workflows:

1. Startup / SAFE Raise
2. Local Business Expansion Raise

The local business mode is intended for owners such as contractors, roofers, HVAC operators, cleaning companies, trucking operators, tradespeople, and other local service businesses raising for equipment, vehicles, crews, materials, working capital, or expansion.

## Important limitation

The exported static portal is a presentation and intake-start page. It can prepare an email to the owner, but it does not include a backend database. Direct submission capture into the owner dashboard requires a later hosted/backend version.

## Packaging

Source-only package. Do not include node_modules or dist.
