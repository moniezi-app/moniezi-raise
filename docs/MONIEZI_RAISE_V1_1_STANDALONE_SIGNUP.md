# MONIEZI Raise V1.1 — Standalone Raise Setup + Six-Step Investor Signup

This build converts MONIEZI Raise into a focused standalone investor-facing page-app.

## Included

- Raise Setup for company profile, founder contact, round terms, SAFE/private raise details, use of funds, acknowledgments, and wire-security notice.
- Company profile export/import so future MONIEZI Pro Finance builds can transfer company details into Raise.
- Six-step investor signup flow:
  1. Investor Type
  2. Contact Details
  3. Entity Information
  4. Investment Interest / Proposed Pledge
  5. Investor Qualification
  6. Review, Download & Submit
- Downloadable investor package as a standalone HTML review file that can be opened in a browser and printed/saved to PDF.
- Investor submission tracker with status updates and CSV/JSON exports.
- Mobile bottom navigation focused only on Raise workflows.

## Product positioning

MONIEZI Pro Finance remains the owner/admin business finance and internal fundraising tracker. MONIEZI Raise is the standalone private investor-signup and reservation page-app.

## Compliance language

The app uses indication-of-interest/reservation wording. It does not claim to issue securities, complete a sale, or replace legal review, final documents, payment verification, or securities-law compliance.
